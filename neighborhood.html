A Great Location to Live and Play

Nestled between UMKC and Rockhurst Universities, the neighborhood has a vibrant and active feel.

The neighborhood - Troostwood - is the perfect mix of young college students and growing families. There is a strong sense of community here. From the yearly popular block party, to the frequent get-togethers, it is very easy to get to know the neighbors!

Centrally Located

You are just minutes from many of the best places to explore in Kansas City:

  • The Plaza
  • The Nelson Atkins Museum of Art
  • Loose Park
  • Brookside

Plus, with easy access to the US-71 highway, you have direct access to the downtown and river market areas, as well as southern Kansas City.

This location is literally at the heart of Kansas City
